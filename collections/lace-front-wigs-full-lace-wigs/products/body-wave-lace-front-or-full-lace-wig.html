Body Wave Wig

Our Body Wave Wig is full of body. Leave it wavy or straighten it out. Our 100% human lace front and full lace wigs can be coloured, styled and parted like your own hair.

Texture:Body Wave
Colour:Natural
Lace:Lace Front or Full Lace
Hairline: Pre-plucked with baby hairs

Lace Front Wig

Constructed with a 13 x 4 frontal piece and wefts at the back. It can be parted from ear to ear across the pre-plucked natural hairline. Our Lace Front Wigs come with adjustable straps and combs.

Full Lace Wig

This wig can be parted anywhere. With this wig, you can do ponytails, braids and many other versatilestyles. Our Full Lace wigs are do not have clips or straps however they can be added.

More

Treat this hair as if it were your own. Feel free to wash, curl, straighten and style it however you want.
